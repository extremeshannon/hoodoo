What we do

Production-grade apparel and branded goods, start to finish.

  • Cut & sew

    Full cut & sew shop for custom garments, prototypes, and small-to-mid runs with precise construction.

  • Embroidery

    Crisp thread work on hats, jackets, bags, and team gear—tuned for durability and detail.

  • Laser-cut patches

    Clean edges and intricate shapes for morale patches, labels, and layered brand marks.

  • Dye sublimation

    All-over color and photographic prints that bond with polyester for vibrant, long-lasting graphics.

  • Screen printing

    Bold plastisol and water-based inks for tees, hoodies, and promo—dialed in for hand feel and wash fastness.
